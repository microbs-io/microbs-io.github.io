microbs

Microservices Observability

microbs is an open source, vendor-inclusive framework to demo, test, or learn about microservices observability.

Why microbs?

microbs creates immersive and realistic observability experiences:

•Demo - Create interactive observability stories with your apps and tech stack.
•Test - Test your app and tech stack observability under realistic circumstances.
•Learn - Hone your skills in microservices and observability.
